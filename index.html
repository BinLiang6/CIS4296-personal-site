    URP/SSP Student Success Tracker

    The URP/SSP Student Success Tracker is able to consistently keep track of undergradute student success. Previously, the student success was tracked by using Excel. The Director of Undergraduate Research Program will have to email students to get student's success information. The information will then be manually entered into an excel sheet. But, the URP/SSP Student Success Tracker let URP/SSP students update their latest research papers, awards,and presentations. The Director of Undergraduate Research Program, students, and Principal Investigators can easily use this tracker to view and keep track of information.

    Grocery Tracker

    The Grocery Tracker application allows users to store, track, and analyze their purchase history. The user can scan their receipt into the application using their device's camera. Each item's name, cost, and store name will be saved in a secured database. If the user wants to check how much they previously purchased an item for, they can use the search system to find the item. The application will provide the price, store name, location, and date where the item was bought. The user can also search within a time frame to get a sum total on how much money was spent on groceries.

    Mangiamo Pizza Website

    Mangiamo Pizza is for a local mom-and-pop pizzeria. The website gives the customer the option to have their pizza delivered or picked up. There are five sizes from mini to X-large pizza. The customer can choose from a variety of crusts like Regular, Stuffed Crust, Deep Dish, Thin Crust, Cheesy Garlic, and Butter Crust. After selecting the crust, the customer can pick from six types of sauces, Tomato Sauce, Marinara Sauce, Honey BBQ Sauce, Garlic Parmesan Sauce, Alfredo Sauce, and Ranch. Finally, the customer will have a selection of nine different types of toppings to add to their pizza which are Ham, Pepperoni, Bacon, Italian Sausage, Beef, Onions, Black Olives, Pepper, and Mushroom. There is an option for the customer to add premium additions and family meals. When the order is done, the website will present the order receipt to the customer to review.
